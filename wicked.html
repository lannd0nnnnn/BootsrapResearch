Wicked is a musical that reimagines the story of The Wizard of Oz, focusing on the unlikely friendship between Elphaba, the misunderstood "Wicked Witch of the West," and Glinda, the popular "Good Witch of the North." As the two women navigate their lives at Shiz University and the events that lead to their eventual rivalry, the musical explores themes of friendship, identity, and the complexity of good versus evil. With its soaring songs and vibrant characters, Wicked reveals the untold story behind the witches and challenges perceptions of heroism and villainy.
