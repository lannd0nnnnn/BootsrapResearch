Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street is a dark and twisted musical that tells the story of Sweeney Todd, a vengeful barber who returns to London after being wrongfully imprisoned for years. Partnering with Mrs. Lovett, a pie maker, Todd embarks on a murderous spree to exact revenge on the corrupt judge who ruined his life, using his barber shop as the setting for his grisly acts. The musical explores themes of justice, obsession, and moral corruption, blending gruesome violence with sharp wit and memorable songs.
